Who Am I?

Hi I'm Justin Wermske. I am full-time at the Harley-Davidson Motor Company as an Associate Analyst in their IGNITE Global Information Services (GIS) Development Rotational Program. My passions include learning about technology, following technological trends, and educating others on the use of technology. I believe that technology can be utilized to enhance one's learning capacity and provide assistance with daily activities. Ideally, technology provides the opportunity to complete tasks more efficiently. However, technology may present negative consequences of expensive costs, unsupported architecture, and inadequate training due to it's rapidly changing industry. My goal is to utilize my experience to identify inefficiencies in current processes and implement cost-efficient solutions through the use of technology.

In the future, I strive to learn more development languages such as Python and SAP's ABAP. I plan to research technology trends and seek creative ways these trends can be inexpensively implemented within an organization. Most importantly, I seek to strengthen ways of effective communication between the different departments of an organization so miscommunication does not serve as a weakness to an organization.
